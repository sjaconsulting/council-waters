"Tapping the Spring" Web Release

August 11, 2014 · piper

Though the tracks were mastered in 2013, it's been a long year on the road. "Tapping the Spring," the debut album of the Council Waters project, is now available for download. A collaborative effort, Council Waters' music is composed and performed by individuals associated with the RumorZ core collective and released as open content, meaning that you are allowed (even encouraged) to copy, burn, and distribute it for free. To download the songs, simply right-click on the .mp3 link and select "save link as..." from the drop-down menu.
